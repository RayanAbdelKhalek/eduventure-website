Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Eduventure, our VR platform, and how schools can join the program.

Eduventure is a VR-based educational platform designed for schools. It transforms lessons into fully immersive experiences—like walking through a virtual lab, exploring ancient cities, or observing physics experiments in 3D.

Schools can contact us directly through our Partnership Form. We’ll schedule a meeting to discuss your needs and deliver the VR sets to your school once finalized.

We offer ongoing technical support for all partnered schools. If you face any issues, simply reach out through the Support Page, and our team will assist you immediately.
