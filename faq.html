Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Edu-Venture, our VR platform, and how schools can join the program.

Edu-Venture is a VR-based educational platform designed for schools. It transforms lessons into fully immersive experiences, like walking through a virtual lab, exploring real-life examples, or observing physics experiments in 3D.

No, Edu-Venture is not a public app. It's provided exclusively to schools and institutions that partner with us. Each package includes pre-configured VR headsets with the platform already installed.

Schools can contact us directly through our Partnership Form. We'll schedule a meeting to discuss your needs and deliver the VR sets to your school once finalized.

We offer ongoing technical support for all partnered schools. If you face any issues, simply reach out through the Support Page, and our team will assist you immediately.

Edu-Venture is compatible with VR headsets that have an android operating system. For optimal performance, we recommend using the VR headsets we give you for high resolution and motion tracking capabilities and for the app to run smoothly as it is intended for and developed based on these devices.
