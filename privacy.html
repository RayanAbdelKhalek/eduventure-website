Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. Here's how we handle your data responsibly and transparently.

Effective Date: 7/23/2024

1. Information We Collect

Eduventure collects minimal information required to operate the platform securely — such as device identifiers, connection data, and anonymous usage statistics. We do not collect personal student data without explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data solely to improve system performance, ensure security, and provide customer support. We never sell or trade user information for commercial gain.

3. Data Ownership and Control

All educational content, lesson data, and institutional materials belong entirely to the school or organization. Eduventure acts only as a technology provider, not a data owner.

4. Data Security

We employ strict security practices including encryption, local data handling, and secure cloud backups. Only authorized Eduventure team members may access system data for maintenance purposes.

5. School Partnerships

When partnering with Eduventure, schools may provide necessary administrative contact details. These are used exclusively for account setup, communication, and technical assistance.

6. Third-Party Services

Eduventure may integrate secure third-party systems for analytics or authentication. These services comply with industry privacy standards and do not have access to student data.

7. Your Rights

Users can request information about stored data, ask for corrections, or request deletion by contacting us directly at contact@myeduventure.com.

8. Data Retention

Eduventure retains system data only as long as necessary for service performance or legal compliance. When a school ends its partnership, all related records are securely deleted.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Updates will be posted here with a revised “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, contact us at contact@myeduventure.com.

Your privacy, our promise.

Eduventure was built to protect both educators and students. We only collect what's necessary — nothing more.
