What's New in Each Version

Explore the features and improvements in each Edu-Venture edition



Version 1: Foundation

Interactive VR Labs

Initial interactive VR science lessons covering chemistry, physics, and biology topics, students can do experiments safely infont of them.

Math Room

Students can understand 3D geometry in 3D space, and see real-life examples for each shape, get their volume, see them fold and unfold in 3D space.

Offline Ready

Run all lessons with no internet for classroom reliability. And all the App's data is saved locally on the VR Device

Multi-language

Every word, sentence, text, button is translated in English and French to fit all Lebanese Schools.

Version 2: Expansion

+15 More Lessons

Up to 15 more lessons have been added covering a lot of chapters in physics, chemistry, math, biology, geography, and more...

Extreme Experiences

Made 2 realistic adventures students can experience safely, which are a nuclear bomb explosion on the Nuclear Fission and Fusion lesson in physics, and a Volcano Eruption in the Volcano Lesson.

Bug Fixes

Tested and reviewed all the app's code and systems and patched all the bugs and glitches found so the app keeps running smoothly and securely.

New Pedigree

A new pedigree system has been added for easier and smoother lesson choosing, which works on Cycles instead of Grades to fit every school's curriculum.
